General info

I am a 26 years old web developer and coder. I love technology, i love coding, i love music and sport. I love gadgets. I can't imagine my life without my smartphone. I am Android fan. I prefer Linux over Windows. Never used Mac. I love PS4 more than xBox and i support Juventus. Forza Juve! I am a passionated coder from Serbia living on internet. I got interested into coding since i was 9 years old but i never really had opportunity or my parents never had resources to pay quality education for me so everything i know i learned right from you guys! Gotta love the true power of The Internet!

Coding Direction

When i was 9 years old i started with small language called Basic. I had my first old computer that had huge keyboard and green monitor. It could save data on music tape! It was capable of writing some small and fun programs. I didn't know English well back then and my first coding steps were with a help of my dad, who also didn't know anything about coding back then. He still doesn't. I owe him a huge "Thank you!" for making my mind interested into programming. Later we got Playstation 1 so i never really continued coding. Although i did miss it. It wasn't long till i got into a highschool and continued coding. After highschool i dropped college and decided to learn what i love. So here i am today being able to write websites and softwares in many languages such as Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and others. Coding it's my passion, i live though it and i will always have abnormal urge to keep learning and keep being better and better.

Just to mention. I am sitting in a park, connected to Gear VR and coding this website on my Galaxy S7.

Design

You can't develop a good software without good artwork right!? I never believed for myself that i have a so called "talent" for design, colors, creativity and other stuff that seemed boring for me at first. Later on I've realised that i need it as much as i need air so i learned some stuff here and there. Now i am able to design almost anything that comes to my mind. I gotta give some credit to my very close friend who is uber good at designing stuff. He is a platinum designer on 99designs. You can check out his profile and hire him here.

I like getting stuck. Because then i can.

1. Research and learn new stuff on the way.
2. Be lazy untill i come up with idea of course
3. Be mad for not being good enough, which increase my urge to learn more
4. Be happy when i fix the error. Oh, there are no words to describe this heavenly feeling.
